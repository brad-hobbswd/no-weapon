NO WEAPON

The Story Behind the Song That Still Carries the Church
By Bradley Hobbs

Some songs are remembered for a season. Others become part of the language of the Church. This manuscript explores the enduring influence of a declaration of faith that continues to strengthen believers across generations through worship, Scripture, testimony, and hope.
