About the Author

Bradley Hobbs

Christian Author
Educator
Writer

Stories have always shaped the Church. Long before sermons were printed and books filled library shelves, believers carried truth through testimony, worship, and the faithful retelling of God's promises. Those stories continue to inspire every generation, reminding ordinary people that extraordinary faith is often discovered in ordinary moments.

Bradley Hobbs writes Christian literature that explores the intersection of Scripture, worship, testimony, and everyday life. His work reflects a deep appreciation for the songs, passages, and moments that quietly become part of the spiritual language of believers long after the final note has faded.

His writing seeks to preserve the beauty of Christian worship while inviting readers to slow down, reflect, and rediscover timeless truths through immersive storytelling and thoughtful historical reflection.

Featured Literary Work

NO WEAPON

The Story Behind the Song That Still Carries the Church

An independent literary tribute exploring the enduring ministry, cultural influence, and spiritual legacy inspired by one of gospel music's most recognizable declarations of faith. Through narrative storytelling and Christian reflection, the manuscript follows the remarkable journey of a song that continues to strengthen believers across generations.

Author's Reflection

Some songs become memories. Some become movements. Every once in a generation, one becomes part of the language of faith. The greatest stories are not measured by applause but by the quiet way they continue changing lives long after they have been shared.

Thank you for reading and supporting independent Christian literature. May these pages encourage deeper reflection, greater appreciation for worship, and renewed confidence in the enduring promises of God.
